Category:Dungeons

Dungeons can be found all over the world. You will find them in cities, in the country, and everywhere between, and no two are the same. There are several different types of dungeons, including: City Sewers, Caverns, Roc Nests, Groves, Dojos, Dragon Lairs and Sunken Cities.

These are harrowing places, and for your protection, you will not be allowed to venture into them until you reach a certain level. Even then, survival can be a challenge.

To enter any kind of dungeon you will need 20 Refined Oil and 1 Wood. This will create a Torch. If you are a Platinum Member you will need only 10 Refined Oil.

The torch will not last forever. It will burn out when you leave or when you have finished the dungeon.

After you finish a dungeon, simply tap yourself and hit 'Exit'
