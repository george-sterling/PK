Cavern

Caverns are deep, dark dungeons. Of all the dungeons, they are the easiest, but that isn't to say they aren't still a challenge. Within these caverns await many dangers; Troodonts don't take kindly to invaders in their midst. When you happen upon them, they will quickly barricade the doors, and you will not be allowed to leave until you have dispatched of them. If you survive the labyrinth, you may happen upon the Troodont King. Defeat him, and you will be able to collect one of his glistening green Troodont Scales.

Requirements

  • 20 Refined Oil
  • 1 Wood
  • Level 5 Character


Unique Items
Rare Troodont Scales are found in the depths of the Cavern which will unlock the Troodont Doctrine skill.
