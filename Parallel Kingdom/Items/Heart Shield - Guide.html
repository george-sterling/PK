Heart Shield


Protect yourself with the size of your heart. This wonderful heart shaped shield will melt the hatred from your enemies… so that you can stab them. Functions like a regular Shield.

Original Cost: 325 Food

This limited edition item was only available for a short period of time around Valentine's Day 2010. No more of this item will be created in PK, but you may be able to find them in Trade Posts if you really want one.
