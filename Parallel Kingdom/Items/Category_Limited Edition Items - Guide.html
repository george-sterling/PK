Category:Limited Edition Items

Limited edition items come in a variety of types, but they're all items that have unique ways to be obtained and created. The majority are from Swag Events, in which items are up for sale in Trade Posts for a limited time, costing a bit of Food. Some are from unique events, or challenges.

A full list of Limited Edition Events can be found here.

This is a list of all available Limited Edition Items.
