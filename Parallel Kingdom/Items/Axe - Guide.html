Axe

Axes are a useful item for attacking. Found frequently, they are good to have on hand when taking out your rival's structures. Wielding an axe is slightly more encumbering than other weapons.


What to look for:
Various critters carry axes. Most often found on Trolls, but you will also find them dropped by Larvae and Dragons. Any time you attack a critter wielding a weapon of any kind, you are in for taking more damage, so keep a Mourning Berry or two handy. As with all weapons, Axes have a finite lifespan before they wear out, expressed as a percentage. Their life can be extended by repairing the weapon with Halvar's Resin.

Weight: 1.0 kg
Dump value: based on wear and upgrade


Crafting Requirements:

  • 1 Wood
  • 2 Iron
  • Weapon Smithing Level 2

Encumbrance: 20

To see the best weapons/armor for each opponent, see Most Effective Weapon And Armor.
