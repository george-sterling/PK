Metallurgy

Level 1 (cost: 1 Skill Point)

  • Can craft Ice Metal and Banish Metal
  • Can convert 7 Iron Slag into 1 Iron Bar
  • 1% Chance of Creating 1 Iron Slag every time a +0 Weapon or Armor is dumped. (Magnetic boots included)


Level 2 (cost: 1 Skill Point)

  • Can craft Poison Metal and Shade Metal
  • Can convert 5 Iron Slag into 1 Iron Bar
  • 2% Chance of Creating 1 Iron Slag every time a +0 Weapon or Armor is dumped.


Level 3 (cost: 2 Skill Points)

  • Can craft Honed Metal, Dead Metal and Fire Metal
  • Can convert 3 Iron Slag into 1 Iron Bar
  • 3% Chance of Creating 1 Iron Slag every time a +0 Weapon or Armor is dumped.
