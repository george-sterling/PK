Troll Lore

Requirement: To unlock this skill you must give collect 3 Roc Eggs from Roc Nests and give the eggs to a Troll Hut.


Level 1 (cost: 1 Skill Point)

  • Visit Troll Huts as if they were your flag


Level 2 (cost: 1 Skill Point)

  • Trolls will let you chop Mourning Trees


Level 3 (cost: 2 Skill Points)

  • Adds a Troll Horn to your inventory (Used to summon trolls)
    • Tap the Troll Horn and choose "Summon Troll"
    • Can summon 2 Trolls every 5 minutes (max of 6 trolls out)
    • Summoned Trolls pick berries from Mourning Trees to heal themselves
    • Can tell Summoned Trolls to drop Weapons/Armor
    • Trolls will occasionally free you from nets or spider webs
  • "Make Peace" option appears on all Trolls
